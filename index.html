What is this for?

Moonveil Oracle is for entertainment, journaling, and self-reflection. It offers language for what you may already be feeling.

What should it never promise?

It does not promise love, money, healing, pregnancy, legal outcomes, investment returns, safety decisions, or contact from a specific person.

How should users treat it?

As a reflective prompt. The reading can help users name a feeling, choose a small next step, or journal through a question without treating it as certainty.
