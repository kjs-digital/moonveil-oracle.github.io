# Moonveil Oracle AI Image Prompts

Use these prompts for AI-generated background images. The safest first image is the hero background because it changes the feeling of the site without requiring many assets.

## Recommended Hero Background

```text
Create a cinematic mystical oracle room for a modern online tarot and astrology website.
Scene: a dark wooden table near a tall arched window, moonlight coming through sheer curtains, three blank tarot cards on the table, a brass candle holder, subtle crystals, faint incense smoke, soft dust particles in the air.
Style: premium editorial fantasy realism, atmospheric but clean, elegant, not gothic horror.
Composition: wide landscape website hero background, strong empty space on the left side for large headline text, detailed table and cards on the lower right, no people.
Lighting: cool moonlight mixed with warm candle glow, soft contrast, dreamlike but readable.
Color palette: deep midnight blue, warm ivory, muted gold, soft teal accents, tiny rose highlights.
Constraints: no text, no letters, no watermark, no logo, no readable symbols, no faces, no hands, no skulls, no scary horror elements.
Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2, high resolution.
```

## More Memorable Alternative

```text
Create an otherworldly floating oracle altar suspended in a night sky garden.
Scene: a circular stone table floating above calm water, three luminous tarot cards hovering above it, a crescent moon behind thin clouds, small stars reflected in the water, delicate gold astrological lines in the air.
Style: surreal premium fantasy realism, elegant spiritual wellness brand, cinematic, polished.
Composition: wide landing page background, center-right focal point, left side clean and calm for headline text, no characters.
Lighting: moonlit, glowing cards, gentle mist, soft rim light.
Color palette: midnight navy, moonlit ivory, muted gold, teal, faint rose.
Constraints: no text, no typography, no watermark, no logo, no faces, no hands, no religious symbols, no horror.
Aspect ratio: 16:9, high resolution.
```

## Subtle Texture Background

```text
Create a seamless mystical paper texture for an online oracle reading website.
Visual: warm ivory parchment with very subtle star dust, faint lunar circles, delicate gold constellation lines, soft teal and rose speckles.
Style: elegant modern esoteric stationery, minimal, premium, not busy.
Composition: seamless or tile-friendly background texture, no focal object.
Constraints: no text, no letters, no watermark, no logo, no dark stains, no faces, no hands.
Aspect ratio: 1:1 or 4:3, high resolution.
```
